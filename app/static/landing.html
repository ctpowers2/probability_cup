How it works

Three steps between you and bragging rights

No spreadsheets, no betting slips. Just picks, a model to beat, and an honest scoreboard.

STEP 01

Make your picks

Call the winner of every fixture — favorite, underdog, or a gutsy draw. Change your mind any time before kickoff.

STEP 02

The AI posts its odds

A Dixon-Coles Poisson model rates all 48 teams and prices every match — then explains its reasoning in plain English.

STEP 03

Beat the Brier score

You and the model are graded on the same metric as results land. Lower score wins. Climb the board, humiliate the AI.

Under the hood

A real quant engine, not a coin flip

Most pools guess. Probability Cup runs an actual statistical model — the same kind that prices real betting markets.

Live odds that move

Goals, red cards, the clock winding down — the model re-prices in real time with Bayesian in-play updates. Watch the bar swing the instant the ball hits the net.

Dixon-Coles model

Poisson goal expectations tuned per team, calibrated on live tournament results.

Explainable picks

Every probability comes with a one-line "why" — Elo gap, attack strength, projected scoreline.
